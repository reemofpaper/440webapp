Take a moment and make sure you can handle what is to come. If you have the guts to face the living dead I salute you. Here are the instructions, and good luck!!!

  1. - At the top of the page, click on the "Play Game" link
  2. - Enter a Game Data File (GDF) that correctly follows the format instructions. If you don't have one made yet, play along with the sample provided
  3. - Answer the questions in the game to the best of your ability! You have 4 tries to avoid creating the zombie. Every question wrong get you closer to a zombie following you. Let's try to avoid having nightmares.
  4. - If you get more than 4 questions wrong, you lose!!! Unfortunately if the zombie is made there is no telling what it will do.... I wish you the best on this adventure!!!
